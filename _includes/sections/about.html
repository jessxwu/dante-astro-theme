About

Hi there! Happy for you to have found this page. This is a text-based site to house my writing.

A little bit about me.
I'm 24 years-young and obsessed with the art of capturing the story - here is where I document the story of (my) brain!
I'm a marketer by day, working at one of Canada's largest retailers, Canadian Tire.
Graduate from Schulich with a longtime obsession with stats and data, and consumer psychology.
I moonlight as a creative writer, fitness enthusiast, armchair philosopher, budding cinephile, and music nerd.

This is starting to read like a dating profile, but I'm all in now...
I'm a fearless purveyor of the open web and a sucker for nostalgia, memories, and all things old.
I'm a big advocate of always learning and continuous self-improvement.
I'm also organized as heck (but also sometimes disorganized as heck), and this paradoxical chaos is sort of the paradox of myself.

I love surrounding myself with great people that keep me growing and grounded.
I love adventurous people, someone that says yes most of the time.
I run and I gym and I bike, love staying active and being outdoors. I'm also a big fan of dancing poorly - but passionately!

Self-awareness, discipline, and empathy is big for me. Also, a willingness to get out of your comfort zone, learn and try new things, or at least just have a conversation about it. I'll try anything twice.
I don't take myself too seriously and relish in being a little bit odd.

I'm passionate about waste reduction, economic empowerment, and equality.

I update this page in a sporadic and unceremonious fashion, so no promises that it's up-to-date or organized.
